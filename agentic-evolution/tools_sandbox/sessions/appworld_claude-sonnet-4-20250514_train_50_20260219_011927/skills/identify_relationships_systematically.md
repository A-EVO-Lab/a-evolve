# identify_relationships_systematically

**Scope:** 📚 Strategic Skill

**Description:** Systematic approach to identify relationships between users, considering multiple data points beyond just surnames

**When to use:** identify roommates, find siblings, determine relationships, who is related to, find family members

## Instructions

Step 1: Identify the target user and relationship type from the task.

Step 2: For roommate relationships:
- Do NOT rely solely on surname matching (roommates typically don't share surnames)
- Check Venmo transaction history for frequent payments (rent, utilities, food)
- Look for transaction descriptions mentioning 'rent', 'utilities', 'groceries', 'bills'
- Check transaction frequency and regularity
- Verify mutual transactions (both directions)

Step 3: For sibling relationships:
- Check for shared surnames first
- Look for family-related transaction descriptions
- Verify age proximity if available
- Check for mutual connections to same parents

Step 4: For friend relationships:
- Look for social transaction descriptions ('dinner', 'movie', 'drinks')
- Check frequency of interactions
- Look at mutual friend networks

Step 5: Always compile a COMPLETE list before proceeding:
- Create a list of ALL identified relationships
- Verify each relationship meets the criteria
- Count the total to ensure you haven't missed any

Step 6: Document your findings clearly before taking action.
